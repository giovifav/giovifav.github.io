Jigsaw Puzzle

A relaxing puzzle game where you reassemble images into complete pictures.

Game Overview

The game features puzzles based on photographs, divided into categories like animals, landscapes, architecture, and people. You can choose from different difficulty levels, from a simple 3x3 to a more challenging 8x8.

Download the game

How to Play

  1. Select an image from the menu.
  2. Choose your preferred difficulty level.
  3. Use your mouse or touch to move pieces and zoom for details.
  4. Once completed, you'll see your time and number of moves.
  5. You can resume a saved game or tackle hardcore mode, which has no hints.

Features

  • Categorized Images: Animals, landscapes, architecture, people.
  • Difficulty Levels: From 3x3 to 8x8.
  • Hardcore Mode: A challenge with no hints.
  • Save/Resume Game: You never lose your progress.
  • Leaderboard: Compare your time with other players.
  • Audio Options: Separate controls for music and sound effects.
  • Multilingual Support: Available in Italian and English.
  • Custom Images: Use your own photos to create new puzzles.
  • Optimized Controls: Suitable for both mouse and touch devices.
