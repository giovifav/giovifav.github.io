Whisperli

A minimalist web application for creating your ideal soundscape. It combines different ambient sounds to help you relax, focus, or fall asleep, right from your browser.

What you can do

  • Explore a vast sound library, divided by categories.
  • Create your blend in the intuitive mixer by adding and removing elements.
  • Manage your mix: start, pause, and save your favorite blends.
  • Personalize the experience with various backgrounds and easily switch between light and dark themes.
  • Search for a specific sound with the built-in search function.

Technologies

The application is developed using:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript (ES6 Modules)
